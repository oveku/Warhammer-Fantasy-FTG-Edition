# Character Generation (FTG Edition)

This file describes how to create a character in Warhammer Fantasy FTG Edition.

## 1. Roll Stats
Stats:
WS (Weapon Skill): Melee combat ability (20 + 2d10)
BS (Ballistic Skill): Ranged combat accuracy (20 + 2d10)
S (Strength): Physical power (20 + 2d10)
T (Toughness): Resistance to damage and hardship (20 + 2d10)
Ag (Agility): Speed, reflexes, and dexterity (20 + 2d10)
Int (Intelligence): Reasoning and knowledge (20 + 2d10)
WP (Willpower): Mental fortitude and resistance (20 + 2d10)
Fel (Fellowship): Social skill and charisma (20 + 2d10)

## 2. Choose Race
Choose one of the following races:
- Human: Adaptable and ambitious, dominant in the Old World.
- Dwarf: Stout, resilient, and proud, masters of craft and tradition.
- Elf: Agile, wise, and mysterious, attuned to magic and nature.

## 3. Choose Class

Choose one of the following classes:
- Soldier: Trained in battle and discipline.
- Scout: Skilled in stealth and survival.
- Priest: Channeler of faith and divine power.
- Wizard: Student of arcane arts and magic.
- Merchant: Expert in trade, negotiation, and wealth.
- Outlaw: Rogue living outside the law.
- Noble: Born to privilege, skilled in leadership and intrigue.
- Artisan: Master of craft and creation.
- Hunter: Tracker and slayer of beasts.
- Sailor: Veteran of rivers and seas.

## 5. Talents
 - Choose talents according to your class/career and multiclass options.
Talents are divided into two groups:

### Innate Talents
Talents you are born with or gain from your race/background.
- Night Vision: See in low light conditions
- Hardy: Increased resistance to fatigue and injury
- Coolheaded: Bonus to Willpower tests
- Strong-minded: Resistance to mental effects
 - Animal Affinity: Natural connection with animals
 - Fast Healer: Recover from wounds more quickly
 - Lucky: Reroll one failed test per session
 - Resistant to Disease: Less likely to suffer illness
 - Keen Senses: Improved hearing, sight, or smell
 - Unshakeable: Immune to fear effects
 - Iron Stomach: Can eat spoiled or strange food without ill effect

### Learnable Talents
Talents you can acquire through training or experience.
- Dual Wielder: Use two weapons effectively
- Marksman: Improved accuracy with ranged weapons
- Magical Aptitude: Ability to learn and cast spells
- Leader: Inspire and coordinate allies
 - Battle Trained: Gain one additional attack in combat
 - Veteran Fighter: Gain two additional attacks in combat
 - Master of Battle: Gain three additional attacks in combat

## 6. Fate
- All characters start with 2 Fate Points.
- Dwarf: +1 additional Fate Point (total 3)
- Human: +2 additional Fate Points (total 4)
