# Character Generation (FTG Edition)

This file describes how to create a character in Warhammer Fantasy FTG Edition.

## 1. Roll Stats
- Roll for your starting attributes (FTG baseline: +5 to +10 above standard WFRP).
- Distribute points according to your desired focus (see AI_CONTEXT.md for details).

## 2. Choose Race
- Select your character's race (Human, Dwarf, Elf, Halfling, etc.).

## 3. Choose Class
- Select a starting class/career (Tier 2 level).
- Multiclass: Choose one talent from an adjacent class/career.

## 4. Skills and Talents
- Distribute skill points based on class/career and background.
- Choose talents according to your class/career and multiclass options.

## 5. Fate
- Fate: 1
- Resilience: 1
- Grit: 3-4 (depending on background)

## 6. Gear
- Starting gear tailored to class/career and region.

## 7. Background
- Define your character's background, personal secret, or trait.
- Name, appearance, relationships, goals.

See AI_CONTEXT.md for examples and more detailed house rules.
